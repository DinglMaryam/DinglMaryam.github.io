Saint of the Day

Each day the Church remembers a holy man or woman whose life bears witness to the power of grace — martyrs, monks, theologians, and servants of the poor who show us the many faces of holiness.

This section will feature the saint commemorated on each day of the Ethiopian Orthodox calendar, with their story, feast day, and a brief reflection or prayer in their memory.

✦ EXAMPLE ENTRY

Saint Mary of Zion — Hidar 21

Celebrated as the Ark of the New Covenant, the Theotokos — she who bore God — is commemorated on the 21st of Hidar with great solemnity and processions. Intercede for us, O Pure One.
